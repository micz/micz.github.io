+++ Title = "Thunderdbird Addon: ThunderAI" +++ {{< page_title >}}

ThunderAI is a Thunderbird Addon that uses the capabilities of ChatGPT to enhance email management.
It enables users to analyze, write, correct, and optimize their emails, facilitating more effective and professional communication.
ThunderAI becomes a great tool for anyone looking to improve their email quality, both in content and grammar, making the writing process quicker and more intuitive. There is no need for an API key and is possibile to use this extension even with a free account!
