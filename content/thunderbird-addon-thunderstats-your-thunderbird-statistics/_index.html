Do you remember the old good Eudora statistics? Do you miss them?
Use this addon to statistics to your beloved Thunderbird!


How does it work?
After installing it, open the statistics tab from the Spaces Toolbar or the button at the top of the window.
There are plenty of features and customization options available in the add-on.
You can crunch the statistics for today, yesterday, or for a date range you specify. You can also export the data in CSV format!


Privacy
All data processing is performed locally on your device. No data is transmitted, shared, or stored on any external servers or third-party services. This ensures that your information remains private and secure at all times.
