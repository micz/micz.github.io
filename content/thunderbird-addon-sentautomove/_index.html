SentAutoMove is a Thunderbird addon that automatically organizes your sent emails by moving them to specific folders based on related messages in your mailbox.
Instead of relying on custom rules, SentAutoMove intelligently searches for related conversations and places your sent emails into a subfolder, using a defined prefix, within the folder of the original message.
This way, your communication threads stay neatly organized, making it easier to track and manage your email conversations without any manual intervention.
Enjoy a cleaner, more intuitive way to keep your sent and received emails in sync!
Find out more!


Privacy
All processing is performed locally on your device. No data is transmitted, shared, or stored on any external servers or third-party services. This ensures that your information remains private and secure at all times.
It's requested a full access in Thunderbird as permission, because this addon is using an experimental API to update an IMAP folder after the message move, and there is no ohter way to authorize it.
