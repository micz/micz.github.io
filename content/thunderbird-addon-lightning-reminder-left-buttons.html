Are you using the Lightning Calendar in Thunderbird?
Are you tired to mistakenly press that "Dismiss All" button in the reminder window?
Use this addon to move the Lightning Reminder "Snooze all for" and "Dismiss All" buttons to the left!


How does it work?
Just install it, and everything is done!
